Site Name: Wanderlust Travel Agency

The name "Wanderlust Travel Agency" expresses the spirit of adventure and the desire to explore the world. It is easy to remember and evokes a sense of excitement and discovery.

Site Purpose

The site provides comprehensive travel services including booking flights, hotels, and tours. It also offers travel guides, destination reviews, and a travel blog to inspire and inform visitors.

Scenarios

1. What are the best travel destinations for a family vacation in June?

2. How can I book a customized tour package?
